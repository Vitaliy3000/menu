Quesadilla

Ingredients:

  • 350g chicken fillet
  • 200g onion
  • 120g bell pepper
  • 120g canned corn
  • 140g canned tomatoes
  • 6 tortillas
  • 240 grated cheddar
  • salt and pepper
  • oil for frying

Equipment:

  • Big frying pan for filling
  • Spatula for filling
  • Flat/Grill frying pan for colour
  • Spatula for colour
  • Chop board
  • Knife
  • 6 bowls

Instructions:

1. Cut the onion into cubes.

2. Cut the bell pepper into cubes.

3. Cut the chicken into cubes.

4. Heat pan.

5. Add oil.

6. Fry the onion.

7. Add bell pepper and fry.

8. Add corn and fry.

9. Add tomatoes and fry.

10. Place the filling in a bowl

11. Clean pan

12. Heat pan.

13. Add oil.

14. Fry the chicken

15. Add filling, heat and finish

15. Place the filling in a bowl

16. Place cheese on half of the tortilla,
add filling, another layer of cheese and fold in half

17. Heat flat/grill frying pan

18. Colour tortilla

18. Slice on 2 or 3 pieces

Salmon and mashed potato

Ingredients:

  • 150g salmon
  • 2 slices of lemon
  • salt and pepper
  • oil for frying
  • 250g potatoes for mashed potatoes
  • 500ml water
  • 40g butter
  • 40g milk
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tomato

Equipment:

  • Pot
  • Sieve
  • Knife
  • Peeler
  • Chop board
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Flat/Grill frying pan for colour
  • Spatula for colour

Instructions:

1. Boil water.

2. Salt water, 1tsp on litter.

3. Cut the potatoes 5cm pieces.

4. Boil the potatoes around 15 minutes.

5. Heat milk and butter.

6. Dry the potatoes.

7. Mash the potatoes.

7. Squeeze through sieve.

8. Add butter and milk gradually, mix.

9. Heat frying pan.

10. Cook salmon around 2 minutes on each side.

11. Cut lemon, tomato and cucumber.
