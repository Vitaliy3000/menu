Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions:

1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water.

2. In a separate pan, fry the pancetta with the garlic until crispy.

3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then mix in the cheeses.

4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with the pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat.

5. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring well.

6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Serve immediately.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 2 onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh coriander

Instructions:

1. Dice the chicken breast and season with salt and pepper.

2. Fry the onions, garlic, and ginger paste until golden brown.

3. Add the chicken to the pan and cook until sealed.

4. Stir in the curry powder and cook for another minute.

5. Add the tomatoes and coconut milk, and simmer for 20 minutes.

6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or naan bread.
